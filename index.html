What makes this practice different is range. Most architectural designers spend a career in one discipline. I've operated at a high level across five.

I'm Hasan — an architectural designer based on Long Island with over fifteen years across luxury residential, national retail, commercial infrastructure, and sacred architecture. I started drafting by hand and worked my way through some of the most demanding project environments in the country.

I've designed Hamptons estates for clients who expect perfection, led award-winning retail rollouts directly with corporate design, directed sacred architecture for communities across New York, and I'm now helping define what EV and energy infrastructure should look like as a capital asset class.

That cross-pollination — the discipline of national brand compliance applied to a private residence, the creativity of luxury residential brought into a commercial prototype — is where the real value shows up.

I work directly with clients on every project. No handoffs, no junior staff managing your job. When you hire me, you get me — from the first sketch to the final inspection.
