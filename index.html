ABOUT

Fifteen years. Five disciplines. One point of contact.

Hasan Kaya
Architect · Design Director
New York State Licensed
Suffolk County, Long Island

I'm Hasan — an architect based on Long Island with over 15 years of experience across luxury residential, national retail, commercial infrastructure, and sacred architecture. I started from the bottom, drafting by hand, and worked my way up through some of the most demanding project environments in the country.

I've designed Hamptons estates for clients who expect perfection, managed award-winning Starbucks rollouts directly with NYC Corporate Design, led sacred architecture projects for communities across New York, and now I'm helping define what EV and energy infrastructure should look like as an asset class — not just a utility.

What makes my practice different is range. Most architects spend an entire career in one discipline. I've operated at a high level across five. That cross-pollination — the discipline of national brand compliance applied to a private residence, the creativity of luxury residential brought into a commercial prototype — is where the real value shows up.

I work directly with clients on every project. No handoffs, no junior staff managing your job. When you hire me, you get me — from the first sketch to the final inspection.

I'm also a full-service media and visualization provider for real estate brokers and developers across the Hamptons and Long Island. If it needs to be seen, sold, or approved before it's built, I can produce it.

Licensed in New York State. B.Arch, New York Institute of Technology, 2016. Based in Suffolk County — currently accepting new projects in Long Island and NYC.
